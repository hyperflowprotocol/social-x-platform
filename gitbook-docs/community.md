## 🌟 Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct

**DO**:
- ✅ Be respectful and professional
- ✅ Help newcomers
- ✅ Share knowledge
- ✅ Report scams
- ✅ Provide constructive feedback

**DON'T**:

- Have verified badges
